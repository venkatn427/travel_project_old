RK Beach- Vizag

Ramakrishna Beach also known as R K Beach is situated on the east coast of Bay of Bengal in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh.RK Beach gets its name from the Ramakrishna Mission ashram situated near the beach.

Rushikonda Beach- Vizag

Rushikonda beach is definitely one of the major attractions of Vizag and a paradise for nature lovers. Also an ideal place for enjoying water sports, Rushikonda beach is a must visit place for adventure lovers as well.A trip to Vizag is incomplete without a visit to this beautiful beach of the Port city.During the evening time, this beach is surrounded by nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts the scenic view of the beach with cool breeze caressing them.

Suryalanka Beach- Bapatla

One of the main attractions of Suryalanka Beach is its natural beauty.The best time to holiday in Suryalanka beach is from the months of October to March.The name 'Suryalanka' is derived from the combination of 'Surya' meaning sun and 'Lanka' meaning island, reflecting the beach's stunning sunrises.

Ramapuram Beach -Chirala

Ramapuram Beach is a popular tourist destination located in the Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is situated about 30 kilometers from the city of Vijayawada and is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.It is also a popular spot for bird watching, as it is home to a variety of birds, including pelicans, flamingos, and herons.

Yarada Beach- Vizag

The tourists can participate in various activities at the beach including taking a dip in the sea or making sand castles. One should try the local delicacies which are present at the beach. The deep blue colour of the sea, when merged with the golden glow of the sand, makes the beach appear heavenly, and a trip to the Yarada Beach should be on your list of must-visits.

Bheemili Beach- Vizag

The Bheemunipatnam Beach, at the northern end of the beach road, is a breathtaking sight to behold. Palm beach are trees line the beach, which is a quiet paradise for those seeking solitude.Best time to visit this place is between the months of October and March." The temperature during this time stays between 15 degrees Celsius to 30 degrees Celsius.

Hamsaladeevi Beach- Machilipatnam

The beach is also home to a number of historical and religious sites, including the Hamsaladeevi Temple, a 11th-century Vishnu temple. The beach is also a popular spot for bird watching, as it is home to a variety of birds, including flamingos, pelicans, and herons.Hamsaladeevi Beach is a great place to visit with family and friends. It is a perfect place to relax and escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Kothapatnam Beach- Ongole

Kothapatnam Beach is one amongst the top attractions in Ongole. Located 18 kilometres (11 mi) from the city, the beach is a great source of entertainment for local people and tourists. The clear blue waters of the sea, the long stretches of sand and the gentle breeze mesmerize you. Boating is available here. But for the Karthika Purnima festival, when locals throng in thousands, the beach is less frequented.

Nelaturu Beach -Nellore

Nelaturu Beach is a beautiful rock beach located in the Nellore district.The beach is known for its black sand, clear blue waters, and stunning rock formations.One of the unique features of Nelaturu Beach is its rock formations. The rocks are of different shapes and sizes, and some of them are even carved into the shape of animals and other figures. These rock formations are a popular spot for photography and sightseeing.

Uppada Beach- Kakinada

Uppada Beach is a beautiful beach located near Kakinada in the East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is known for its golden sand, clear blue water, and gentle waves. The beach is also a popular spot for watching the sunrise and sunset.
