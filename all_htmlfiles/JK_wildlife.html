Dachigam National Park

Dachigam National Park is located in the Zabarwan Range of Western Himalayas. Its landscape is dotted with meadows, alpine pastures, scrub vegetation and waterfalls. Some of the wonderful fauna which you can see in this national park of Jammu and Kashmir are musk deer, brown bear, leopards, Himalayan Black Bear and Hangul, the Kashmiri stag. It also houses a rich variety of birds including black bulbuls, colourful pheasants, cinnamon sparrows, pygmy owlet, Himalayan Monal and Kashmiri flycatcher.

Kishtwar High Altitude National Park

Kishtwar High Altitude National Park is located in the Kishtwar district in the Jammu region, whose altitude ranges from 1,700 to 4,800 meters. The amazing fauna found in this park includes Indian Muntjac, Himalayan Tahr, hangul and the snow leopard, which is the main attraction inside the national park. Various bird species like bearded vulture, white cheeked bulbul, griffon vulture and paradise flycatcher, are also found here.

Overa Aru Wildlife Sanctuary

The sanctuary is spread in a wide area and it covers more than 500 sq. km. The sanctuary is home to rare and endangered species such as the musk deer, brown bear and snow leopard. These species are conserved by the state and the central government. Tourists from different parts of the country visit his place for the sight of such beautiful and exotic species of birds and animals.
