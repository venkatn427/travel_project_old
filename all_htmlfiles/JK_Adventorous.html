Trekking

The snow-covered peaks, the cascading streams, the deep, lush green valleys make for the perfect trekking destination. The Shankaracharya hill on the Zabarwan hills offers ideal spots for trekking. The lofty glaciers, the valley filled with rich flora and fauna make your trekking experience even more exciting and engaging.Tourists can hire guides to make the entire journey interesting and informative. The ideal time for trekking starts from July to September.While trekking, adventurers can explore the interiors of Kashmir, filled with its diverse cultural heritage, thrilling history, hilly topography and the scent of deciduous trees.
Location: Kashmir Alpine Lakes Trek

River Rafting

Rafting is one of the most incredible adventure activities in Jammu and Kashmir that you can try during your holidays in Jammu and Kashmir. In Pahalgam, you can battle the rapids of the Lidder River, which presents enough challenge and is exciting. The river stretches from Langabal to Ganeshpora, with rapids ranging from Grade I to Grade IV. While the Lidder Joy Ride, from Varganpal to Yaneer Bridge is perfect for beginners and amateurs, the stretch from Langabal to Ganeshpora is perfect for experts.
Location: The Lidder river in Pahalgam

Skiing

The lofty summits and the shimmering snow provide tourists with the ideal setting to enjoy a winter delight like skiing. Gulmarg is the perfect place for such sport and relishes the game with your heart’s content. Equipment necessary for skiing like skis, sticks, boots, goggles, and gloves is all available in the local Gulmarg ski shops. The ideal time to ski is between December and mid-April. Beginners can get trained by professionals and then set out for skiing. Apart from Gulmarg, one can also enjoy skiing in Pahalgam. Adventurers here can enjoy the winter sun and snow. Tourists can take part in various skiing activities like snowboarding, hockey skiing, and ice skiing. The crystal clear snow-capped hills and the tranquillity of Kashmir calls tourists to visit and explore this abode of snow and ice.
Location: Gulmarg

Paragliding

Paragliding is a famous aero sport adventure activity in Jammu and Kashmir that lets you admire its valleys, meadows & splendid beauty. One of the places to enjoy paragliding is the hill station of Nathatop. Batote and Sanasar are other places where you can enjoy paragliding, from May-June and September-October. Astanmarg, a little village located close to Srinagar, is also famous for paragliding.
Location:Nathatop

Mountain Biking

The Srinagar Leh highway provides the adventure enthusiasts with an opportunity to explore the majestic Himalayas through ten mountain trails. equipment needed for mounting biking include a geared motorcycle, biking helmet, shoes, gloves, insulated and lightweight bottles to store alcoholic drinks, water bags, first aid kit, sunscreen lotion, and sunglasses. It’s advisable to carry a repair kit containing non-toxic degreaser, tubes, grease tubes to grease bearings and an aero spray to clean bike, tool kit, bike pump, speedometer, batteries, maps, etc.
Location: The main areas to go mountain biking include Gulmarg to Pahalgam, Srinagar to Gulmarg, Pahalgam to Srinagar, Srinagar to Sonamarg and then to Wular

Hot Air Ballooning

Jammu and Kashmir is a paradise not only on land but also from the air. And the best way to adore this mesmerizing beauty is from the top, like a bird.The hot air balloon ride takes about 2-3 hours and the takes you to a height of up to 3000 feet. So if you are adventurous and like to enjoy the splendid beauty of Jammu and Kashmir while you are among the clouds, you should definitely try Hot Air Ballooning at the Valleys of Suru and Zanskar. The garb used during trekking is ideal for this as well. So accumulate your shoes, sunglasses, gloves and a cap, and embark upon your excursion, before you are left behind.
Locarion:Valleys of Suru and Zanskar

Jeep Safari

you can hire a driver and a jeep and explore the hidden areas of the Himalayan region through a Jeep Safari. You can travel on the routes like Sonmarg, Gulmarg, Yusmarg, Srinagar and Pahalgam while understanding the real value of Kashmir with its hidden paths and forests, its wildlife and hilltop temples. Kashmir is also well known for its beautiful Shikaras on the Dal lake, its flower valleys and saffron fields which are a must visits when on a jeep safari. The Aru valley, famous due to a Bolloywood film Betab, is also a hotspot for Jeep Safaris.
Location: Aru Valley

Camping At Sonmarg

Nature lovers always enjoy being surrounded by greenery, mountains, flowing streams and a calm environment. If you are one of those and also a bit adventurous, camping at Sonmarg in Jammu and Kashmir which translates into the ‘Meadow of gold’, is one of the best options. You can choose to relax with your friends or family between the months of April-October at one of the many camping sites in this beautiful hill station. From the campsite you can choose to trek to one of the many Himalayan lakes or to places like Zoji-la pass, Baltal and Thajwas Glacier. Most camps also include various activities like fishing, kayaking, canoeing and sports like volleyball. Also, many camps organize bonfires which you can be a part of and feel at peace.
Location: Sonmarg

Mountaineering

The Pir Panjal Ranges of Indian Himalayas provide wonderful opportunities for mountaineering in Jammu and Kashmir, as they are home to some of the best mountain peaks. You can try mountaineering from the Kishtwar region, where the famous peaks are Tupendo & Cerro Kishtwar. Other peaks where climbing expeditions are done are Sickle Moon, Cathedral, Brammah I and II and Namtse. In the Kashmir Valley, the famous mountain climbing peaks are Harmukh, Tattakuti, Sunset & other smaller peaks in Sonamarg and Pahalgam.
Location: Pir Panjal Ranges
